This software is still in beta. If you find any bugs or have a suggestion, send it to info@tunechilla.com

How to use

Play tracks

Single-click on tracks to select them, double-click to play them.

Show options

Right-click on tracks to reveal more options.

Organize your music

Click the heart to add tracks to your library. Drag'n'drop songs to add them to your playlist.
